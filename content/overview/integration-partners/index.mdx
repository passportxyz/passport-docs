## Community integrations

The following partners have published their own guides for integrating with Passport.

### Snapshot

Snapshot is a voting platform that allows DAOs, DeFi protocols, or NFT communities to vote easily and without gas fees.

Snapshot enables you to use Passport in two different ways: Gate access to submitting proposals, and gate access to voting.

<Cards>
  <Cards.Card title="Using Passport with Snapshot" href="https://docs.snapshot.org/space-handbook/voting-threshold#voting-validation" />
</Cards>


### Discourse

Discourse is an open source Internet forum system that is used as a governance tool for many web3 organizations.

There are two different plugins that you can use with Discourse:

<Cards>
  <Cards.Card title="Dappy.lol's Discourse plugin" href="https://meta.discourse.org/t/gitcoin-passport-plugin/273532" />
  <Cards.Card title="Communiteq's Discourse plugin" href="https://github.com/communiteq/discourse-gitcoin-passport" />
</Cards>


### Collab.Land

Collab.Land is a community management tool that supports a wide range of projects, including DAOs, NFT communities, brands, and creators of all sizes.

The Collab.Land integration allows you to gate access to roles to just those users who can prove their humanity on Passport.

<Cards>
  <Cards.Card title="Using Passport with Collab.Land" href="https://docs.collab.land/help-docs/command-center/create-a-tgr/gtc-passport" />
